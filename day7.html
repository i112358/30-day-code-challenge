Short quiz on Taiwan

How much do you know about Taiwan? Test your knowledge or learn something today!

It is an island on the Pacific, east of China, south of Japan, and north of the Philippines.

Taipei City, a city in the northern part of the main island.

The official language is Mandarin, with traditional Chinese as the official script. Other common languages include Hakka and Taiwanese (a sibling of the Hokkien dialect).

Ms. Tsai Ing Wen. (Little fact: She is an alumni of my high school, ZSGH!)

Food! Delicacies such as stinky tofu, beef noodle soup, and the phenomenal bubble tea are all from Taiwan!
